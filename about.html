Our Sound


Drawing from influences like led zeppelin, foo fighters, and arctic monkeys, stagefright pushes boundaries while staying true to the roots of rock. their music is a mix of powerful riffs, haunting melodies, and lyrics that tell stories of love, loss, and everything in between.

Our Mission


Stagefright is committed to creating music that connects with people on a deep level. they believe in the power of live music to bring people together and aim to create a space where fans can lose themselves in the music, if only for a little while.
