Members

StageFright is a dynamic rock band featuring Alex Reed, Maya Torres, Jaxon Lee, and Sophie Nguyen. Blending classic and modern rock influences, they deliver electrifying performances and heartfelt lyrics.

Alex “Axel” Reed
(Lead Guitar/Vocals)


Alex is the heart and soul of StageFright. With his electrifying guitar solos and powerful vocals, he draws fans into every performance. Known for his magnetic stage presence and heartfelt lyrics, Alex blends classic rock vibes with modern influences, creating a sound that resonates deeply with audiences. When he’s not on stage, you might find him jamming with friends or exploring new music.

Maya “Rhythm” Torres
(Bass Guitar)


Maya is the driving force behind StageFright’s infectious grooves. As the bass guitarist, she lays down the foundation that keeps the band locked in sync. Her sharp wit and playful energy make her a crowd favorite, both on and off stage. Maya is passionate about music that moves people, and her love for rhythm shines through in every note. Outside of music, she enjoys photography and skateboarding.

Jaxon “Drumline”
Lee (Drummer)


Jaxon is the heartbeat of StageFright. His high-energy drumming and infectious enthusiasm bring every song to life. A master of rhythm, he knows how to keep the audience engaged and dancing. Jaxon’s versatility allows him to experiment with different styles, ensuring that no two performances are ever the same. When he’s not behind the drums, he loves hiking and discovering new trails.

Sophie “Synth” Nguyen
(Keyboard/Vocals)


Sophie adds a unique layer to StageFright with her captivating synth melodies and harmonies. As the band’s keyboardist and vocalist, she brings a fresh, modern twist to their rock sound. Her creativity and passion for music shine through in every performance. Outside of music, Sophie enjoys painting and experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
